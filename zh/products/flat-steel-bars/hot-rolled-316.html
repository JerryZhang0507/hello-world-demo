产品概述

The 316 stainless steel hot rolled flat bar is a premium molybdenum-alloyed austenitic stainless steel offering significantly enhanced resistance to pitting and crevice corrosion in chloride-rich environments. Containing 16-18% chromium, 10-14% nickel, and 2-3% molybdenum, it outperforms 304 in harsh conditions.

Ideal for marine environments, coastal construction, chemical processing equipment, and pharmaceutical manufacturing where superior corrosion resistance is essential.
