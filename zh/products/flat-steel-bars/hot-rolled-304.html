产品概述

The 304 stainless steel hot rolled flat bar is a versatile, general-purpose austenitic stainless steel product offering excellent corrosion resistance, good formability, and reliable mechanical properties. Hot rolling produces a characteristic scaled surface finish suitable for structural and industrial applications where surface aesthetics are not the primary concern.

Grade 304 is the most widely used stainless steel grade, containing 18% chromium and 8% nickel, providing robust resistance to oxidation and a wide range of chemical environments. Ideal for construction frameworks, machinery components, and general fabrication.
