304不锈钢钢丝绳 7x19结构

The 7x19 construction features 7 strands with 19 wires each, delivering the highest flexibility among standard stainless steel wire rope configurations. Made from grade 304 stainless steel, this rope combines excellent corrosion resistance with the ability to bend smoothly around small-diameter sheaves and pulleys.

Ideal for marine running rigging, sailboat halyards and sheets, mechanical control cables, gym equipment cables, and any application requiring high flexibility with reliable corrosion protection. Available in diameters from 1mm to 16mm. Manufactured to ASTM A1023, GB/T 20118, and EN 12385 standards.
