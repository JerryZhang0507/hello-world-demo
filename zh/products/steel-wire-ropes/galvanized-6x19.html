镀锌钢丝绳 6x19结构

The 6x19 construction is the most widely used wire rope configuration in general lifting and rigging applications. Each strand contains 19 individual wires arranged around a central core, providing an excellent balance of strength, flexibility, and abrasion resistance. Our galvanized finish offers reliable corrosion protection for outdoor and industrial environments.

Available in diameters from 1mm to 40mm with both Fiber Core (FC) and Independent Wire Rope Core (IWRC) options. All ropes are manufactured to ASTM A1023, GB/T 20118, and EN 12385 international standards.
