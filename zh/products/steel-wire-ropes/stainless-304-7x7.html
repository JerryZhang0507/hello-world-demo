304不锈钢钢丝绳 7x7结构

The 7x7 construction features 7 strands, each composed of 7 wires, creating a compact and relatively stiff wire rope ideal for structural and architectural applications. Made from grade 304 stainless steel, this rope offers 优异的耐腐蚀性 and a bright, clean aesthetic finish perfect for visible installations.

Commonly used in balustrade infill, cable railing systems, architectural tension structures, green wall supports, and light-duty lifting. Available in diameters from 1mm to 12mm. Manufactured to ASTM A1023, GB/T 20118, and EN 12385 standards.
