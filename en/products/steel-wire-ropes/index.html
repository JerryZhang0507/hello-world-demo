Stainless Steel & Galvanized Wire Ropes

Cinray Metal supplies a comprehensive range of high-performance steel wire ropes for industrial, marine, architectural, and commercial applications. Our wire ropes are manufactured from premium-grade stainless steel (304, 316) and hot-dip galvanized carbon steel, available in multiple constructions to meet diverse load-bearing and environmental requirements.

Applications

Lifting & Rigging

Overhead cranes, hoists, slings, and general lifting equipment requiring high tensile strength and reliability.

Marine & Offshore

Mooring lines, ship rigging, offshore platform cables, and dock fittings resistant to saltwater corrosion.

Architectural

Balustrade infill, cable railings, tension structures, green wall support, and decorative architectural features.

Mining

Hoist ropes, haulage cables, and guide ropes for mining operations in demanding underground conditions.

Fishing & Aquaculture

Trawl cables, aquaculture cage nets, and marine farming infrastructure requiring long-term seawater exposure resistance.

Industrial Machinery

Conveyor systems, elevator cables, winch lines, and mechanical control cables for industrial automation.
