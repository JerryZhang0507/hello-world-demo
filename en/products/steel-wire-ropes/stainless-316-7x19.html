316 Stainless Steel Wire Rope 7x19 Construction

The 316 stainless steel 7x19 wire rope represents our premium offering, combining the high flexibility of 7x19 construction with the superior corrosion resistance of grade 316 stainless steel. The addition of molybdenum in 316 grade provides exceptional resistance to chloride environments, making this rope the definitive choice for the most demanding marine and chemical applications.

Engineered for offshore platforms, seawater-exposed rigging, chemical processing equipment, food-grade installations, and coastal architectural projects where standard 304 stainless steel may not provide sufficient long-term corrosion protection. Available in diameters from 1mm to 16mm. Manufactured to ASTM A1023, GB/T 20118, and EN 12385 standards.

Key Features

Superior Corrosion Resistance

316 grade with molybdenum content provides exceptional resistance to chlorides, saltwater, acids, and harsh chemical environments.

High Flexibility

7x19 construction ensures smooth operation over pulleys and sheaves with minimal bending fatigue, even in corrosive environments.

Offshore-Grade Performance

Specified for offshore platforms, marine mooring, desalination plants, and coastal infrastructure where 304 grade is insufficient.

Food-Grade & Pharmaceutical

316 stainless steel meets stringent hygiene requirements for food processing, pharmaceutical manufacturing, and cleanroom applications.
