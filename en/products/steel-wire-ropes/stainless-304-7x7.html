304 Stainless Steel Wire Rope 7x7 Construction

The 7x7 construction features 7 strands, each composed of 7 wires, creating a compact and relatively stiff wire rope ideal for structural and architectural applications. Made from grade 304 stainless steel, this rope offers excellent corrosion resistance and a bright, clean aesthetic finish perfect for visible installations.

Commonly used in balustrade infill, cable railing systems, architectural tension structures, green wall supports, and light-duty lifting. Available in diameters from 1mm to 12mm. Manufactured to ASTM A1023, GB/T 20118, and EN 12385 standards.

Key Features

Excellent Corrosion Resistance

304 stainless steel provides outstanding resistance to oxidation, moisture, and mild chemical exposure, suitable for indoor and outdoor use.

Aesthetic Appearance

Bright polished finish delivers a clean, modern look ideal for architectural applications where the wire rope is visible.

Compact & Stiff Design

7x7 construction provides minimal stretch and higher stiffness, ideal for structural tension members and fixed rigging.

Smooth Surface

Fewer outer wires create a smoother surface that is gentle on hands and fittings, important for architectural handrails and balustrades.
