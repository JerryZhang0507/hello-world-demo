304 Stainless Steel Wire Rope 7x19 Construction

The 7x19 construction features 7 strands with 19 wires each, delivering the highest flexibility among standard stainless steel wire rope configurations. Made from grade 304 stainless steel, this rope combines excellent corrosion resistance with the ability to bend smoothly around small-diameter sheaves and pulleys.

Ideal for marine running rigging, sailboat halyards and sheets, mechanical control cables, gym equipment cables, and any application requiring high flexibility with reliable corrosion protection. Available in diameters from 1mm to 16mm. Manufactured to ASTM A1023, GB/T 20118, and EN 12385 standards.

Key Features

Maximum Flexibility

19 wires per strand provide the highest flexibility in the 7-strand family, enabling smooth operation over small sheaves and pulleys.

Corrosion Resistant

304 stainless steel provides reliable protection against rust and oxidation in marine, outdoor, and humid environments.

Smooth Running Surface

Fine wire construction creates a smooth outer profile that reduces friction and wear on pulleys, sheaves, and contact surfaces.

Versatile Applications

Suitable for running rigging, control cables, gym equipment, garage door cables, and any application where the rope must bend frequently.
