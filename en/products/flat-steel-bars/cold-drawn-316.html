Product Overview

The 316 stainless steel cold drawn flat bar combines the superior corrosion resistance of molybdenum-alloyed 316 grade with the precision, surface quality, and mechanical properties of the cold drawing process. This product delivers exceptional performance in demanding environments where both dimensional accuracy and chloride-corrosion resistance are required.

Perfect for marine precision components, chemical processing equipment internals, and high-end architectural fittings where a premium finish and reliable long-term performance in corrosive environments are non-negotiable.
