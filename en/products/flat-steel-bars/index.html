Premium Stainless Steel Flat Bars for Global Export

Cinray Metal supplies high-quality stainless steel flat bars in a comprehensive range of sizes, grades, and finishes. Our flat bars are manufactured to international standards and are suitable for structural, industrial, and decorative applications. With thickness ranging from 3mm to 30mm and width from 20mm to 200mm, we offer both hot-rolled and cold-drawn variants in grades 304, 316, and 316L to meet diverse project requirements.

Each product undergoes rigorous quality inspection before shipment, ensuring consistent dimensional accuracy, surface finish, and mechanical properties. Custom lengths and special tolerances are available upon request.

Common Applications

Stainless steel flat bars serve critical roles across industries

Construction

Structural supports, bracing, frames, and architectural trim in buildings and bridges.

Machinery

Machine components, tooling parts, conveyor rails, and industrial equipment frames.

Marine

Shipbuilding fittings, dock hardware, offshore platform components, and saltwater-exposed structures.

Automotive

Chassis components, exhaust brackets, trim pieces, and fabrication parts for vehicles.
